Frequently Asked Questions:

1) How much is the minimum amount for a transaction to occur? - A minimum of 10 pesos (10 PHP) is required in order to create an invoice for the transaction.

2) Do you support other forms of electronic payment like bank transfers? - We only accept electronic payments made using Gcash or Maya.

3) Is it necessary to enter my mobile number in the payment form? - No. However, if you wish to receive a sms of your transaction, it is necessary to enter the recipient's mobile no.

4) Can I pay using eWallet and coins under the same transaction? - Unfortunately, the vending machine does not support different payment options under a single transaction.

5) I am interested on this vending machine product, who should I reach out? - You may email us at @ apscservices99@gmail.com.
